Travel Credit Card

Definition

A Travel Credit Card is tailored for frequent travelers, offering rewards, discounts, and perks on flight bookings, hotel stays, and international spending.

Features

  • Earn air miles or reward points on travel-related purchases
  • Airport lounge access (domestic/international)
  • Exclusive travel partner discounts
  • No foreign transaction fees (on select cards)

Benefits

  • Ideal for frequent flyers and international tourists
  • Hotel upgrades and travel insurance coverage
  • Faster accumulation of miles for free tickets
  • Concierge services and 24x7 travel assistance

Monthly Allowance

  • Credit Limit: ₹30,000 - ₹1,00,000 (based on income)
  • Miles Earned: 1 - 4 miles per ₹100 spent (category dependent)
  • Interest-Free Period: Up to 50 days
  • Access to 1–4 complimentary lounge visits/month
