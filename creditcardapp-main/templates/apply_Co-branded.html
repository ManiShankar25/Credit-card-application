Co-branded Credit Card

Definition

A Co-branded Credit Card is a partnership between a credit card issuer and a company, offering rewards and benefits specific to that brand or company.

Features

  • Brand-specific rewards and discounts
  • Access to partner offers and deals
  • Exclusive membership benefits
  • Custom designs and branding

Benefits

  • Rewards for spending within the brand
  • Special offers and exclusive deals
  • Easy access to brand-related perks

Monthly Allowance

  • Credit Limit: ₹30,000 - ₹75,000
  • Cash Withdrawal Limit: 25% of credit limit
  • Interest-Free Period: Up to 45 days
